Help & Support

Q: How do I add my availability?

Go to the Dashboard and click "Add Availability" or navigate to the Negotiate tab. Fill in your date, start time, end time, and priority level. Your slots will appear on the calendar immediately.

Q: How do I create a meeting request?

Click "New Request" from the Dashboard or Negotiate tab. Enter the meeting title, your name, preferred date and time. The system will add this to pending requests.

Q: What does "Run Negotiation" do?

The AI algorithm analyzes all meeting requests and available slots to find the best matches. It considers exact time matches, proximity, priority levels, and provides match scores to help you make decisions quickly.

Q: What is the match score?

The match score (0-100%) represents how well a slot matches a request. Higher scores indicate better compatibility based on date, time overlap, and priority. Scores above 70% are recommended for confirmation.

Q: Can I view my calendar?

Yes! Click the Calendar tab to see a weekly view of all your meetings and availability slots. Use the navigation arrows to browse different weeks or click "Today" to return to the current week.

About This App

Smart Calendar Negotiator is an intelligent scheduling application designed to help teams find optimal meeting times. Using advanced AI algorithms, it analyzes meeting requests and available slots to provide the best possible matches, saving time and reducing scheduling conflicts.
